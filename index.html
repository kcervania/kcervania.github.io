About
Kathleen was born and raised in the suburbs of Dubai. Her career took off at the age of 16. She worked as a Virtual Assistant and in a Print shop while going to college for Graphic Design. She took on different roles from: Head of Media, to Marketing Associate, then a Graphic Designer for a BPO. She designed Web Ads and got recognized for an ad that generated 5,000 clicks within 24 hours. After a few years, Kathleen got an opportunity to be an Interactive Designer in Malaysia. Before heading to Calgary, she moved to Timor Leste for a few months, where she started freelancing and scuba diving. Kathleen's career peaked when she started freelancing, while it took her a good 2 years to get great clients locally and internationally. Her career is beginning a new chapter.
